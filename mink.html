Minks

fun facts:

1. Minks have a slender body with short legs, long neck, rounded head and small ears.

2. They have webbed feet!

3. They're covered with an oily substance that prevents their fur from soaking.

4. These features show their adaption to life in the water. 😱

5. Minks are also nocturnal animals and skillful hunters! They eat fish 🐟, frogs 🐸 and insects 🦗.

More fun facts!

  1. They are located in Northern America and Europe
  2. Sometimes they are found living in abandoned beaver dams!
  3. Minks usually live for 3 years in the wild and 10-12 years in captivity. In their lifetime, minks usually have 4-6 offspring.
  • Don't be fooled by their cute looks! They will attack any other mink that enters their territory! 👀
  • Although, some males have been known to tolerate females in their territory.

- Minks are also killed in March and frozen for their pelts/fur in the winter! 😦
- They are often treat poorly by handlers and there have even been reports of up to 70 minks being thrown into cages and being gassed.
- Coats made of mink fur are often expensive, but for what cost? 🤨 Luckily, they are not endangered, as they often birth in large numbers!
